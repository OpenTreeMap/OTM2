OpenTreeMap uses the i-Tree Streets software developed by the United States Forest Service to calculate the ecosystem benefits of trees. i-Tree Streets is not recommended for use outside the United States, and thus we are unable to show ecosystem information for trees in other countries.

If you are interested in integrating a different ecosystem calculation system into OpenTreeMap or have information on other methods for calculating eco benefits, please contact us at {{ "opentreemap@publicservicerequest.com"|urlize }}.
